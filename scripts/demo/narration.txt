This is Match Pulse Rooms: a second screen that helps football fans feel the match and understand why.

Live-score apps tell you what happened. Betting apps show a price. Match Pulse turns scores, match events, and market movement into one transparent momentum story.

The Pulse is not a win probability or a betting tip. Every movement is explainable. Territory, attacking threat, market shift, and recent events each show their contribution, so the fan can challenge the signal instead of trusting a black box.

The interactive replay makes the product judgeable at any time. As the match changes, the score, Pulse, Tension, chart, and room narrative move together.

Fans can make a free full-time call with no wallet, stake, token, or prize. Their local choice becomes a social signal, and the crowd-versus-market view makes disagreement part of the experience.

Every swing is connected to a moment on the timeline. The same normalized engine can power publisher embeds, club communities, sponsor-safe moments, stadium screens, and creator rooms.

For live data, the browser sends only a fixture ID to our backend. The server obtains a short-lived guest session, adds the protected TxLINE subscription token, fetches scores and odds, and returns a narrow room model. Credentials and raw feeds never reach the browser.

Match Pulse Rooms turns sports data into an explainable fan experience: no wagering, just a better way to read the game together.
